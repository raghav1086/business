## Content Strategy Overview

### Content Pillars

1. **Education** (40%): GST tips, business advice, accounting basics
2. **Inspiration** (25%): Success stories, Samruddhi philosophy, values
3. **Product** (20%): Feature highlights, how-to guides, updates
4. **Community** (10%): User stories, testimonials, engagement
5. **Cultural** (5%): Indian business values, festivals, traditions

### Posting Frequency

- **LinkedIn**: 3-4 posts per week
- **Twitter/X**: 1-2 posts per day
- **Facebook**: 1 post per day
- **Instagram**: 1 post per day + 3-5 stories per week

## Measurement & Optimization

### Key Metrics

- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares
- **Reach**: How many people saw the post
- **Click-Through Rate**: Links clicked
- **Follower Growth**: New followers gained
- **Conversion**: Sign-ups from social media

### A/B Testing

- Test different headlines
- Test different images
- Test posting times
- Test hashtag combinations
- Test CTA wording

## Content Approval Checklist

Before posting, ensure:

- [ ] Aligns with Samruddhi philosophy
